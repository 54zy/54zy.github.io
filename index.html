Biography

I am an Associate Professor at the College of Artificial Intelligence, Nanjing University of Aeronautics and Astronautics (NUAA), and a member of the iBrain Group. I received my Ph.D. from the State Key Laboratory of Cognitive Intelligence, University of Science and Technology of China (USTC), where I was advised by Prof. Qi Liu.

My research interests include Large Language Models, AI for Education, Machine Learning, and Measurement Theory. My work is centered on two complementary directions: enhancing human cognition with AI and improving intelligent systems through human cognitive and measurement mechanisms. Specifically, I study AI models and adaptive assessment methods for educational scenarios, with applications in large-scale online learning platforms such as the Open University of China. I also investigate reliable training and evaluation frameworks for LLMs and intelligent agents from the perspective of human assessment and cognitive measurement.
