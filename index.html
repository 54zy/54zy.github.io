Biography

I'm a PhD student in BASE Group, University of Science and Technology of China (USTC). My advisors is Prof. Qi Liu.

My research mainly focuses on AI for Education, including Computerized Adaptive Testing, Cognitive Diagnosis, Student Modeling. Recently I am focusing on the Evaluation and Diagnosis for Large Language Model
